Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InvoiceEase

Getting Started

How do I create my first invoice? +
After signing up, simply send a WhatsApp message to our number in this format: "Invoice for [Client Name], ₹[Amount], [Service Description]". For example: "Invoice for Acme Corp, ₹50,000, Website Design". You'll receive a GST-compliant PDF within 30 seconds!
Do I need to download an app? +
No! That's the beauty of InvoiceEase. Everything works through WhatsApp, which you already have on your phone. Just save our WhatsApp number and start sending invoice requests.
What information do I need to provide during signup? +
You'll need: Email, WhatsApp number, Business name, and optionally your GST number, PAN, address, and bank details. The more you provide upfront, the more automatic your invoices become. But you can always add this information later in your settings.

Using InvoiceEase

Can I send invoice requests in Hindi? +
Yes! You can send invoice requests in Hindi, Marathi, or English. Our AI understands all three languages. You can even use voice notes! For example: "भैया, Sharma ji के लिए invoice बना दो, 50 हज़ार रुपये, website के लिए"
How do I add multiple items to one invoice? +
Simply list all items in your WhatsApp message. For example: "Invoice for ABC Ltd, Item 1: ₹20K website design, Item 2: ₹10K SEO services, Item 3: ₹5K content writing". Our AI will parse each item and create a detailed invoice.
Can I customize the invoice template? +
Pro and Business users get access to 3 professional templates and can upload their logo. You can switch templates anytime from your dashboard. Free users get one basic template.
Where can I see all my past invoices? +
Log in to your dashboard at invoiceease.in to see all your invoices. You can download any invoice as PDF, mark them as paid/unpaid, and see analytics about your billing.
Can I edit an invoice after it's created? +
Currently, you cannot edit an invoice after creation (for accounting integrity). If you made a mistake, simply create a new invoice with the correct information. This also doesn't count against your monthly limit on paid plans.

GST & Compliance

Are the invoices GST compliant? +
Yes! All invoices are 100% GST compliant as per Indian tax regulations. They include CGST/SGST breakdown, invoice numbering, GST numbers, and all required fields.
What if I don't have a GST number? +
No problem! If you're not registered for GST (turnover under ₹40 lakh), you can still generate invoices. The GST field will simply be marked as N/A, and no GST will be calculated. Your invoices will still be professional and legally valid.
How is GST calculated? +
For services, we apply 18% GST (9% CGST + 9% SGST) for intra-state transactions and 18% IGST for inter-state. The invoice clearly shows the breakdown. You can customize GST rates in your settings if needed.

Billing & Payments

How does the Free plan work? +
The Free plan gives you 5 invoices per month, forever. No credit card required. Perfect for trying out InvoiceEase or if you invoice infrequently. When you hit the limit, you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited invoices.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, you'll automatically move to the Free plan.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with InvoiceEase for any reason, email us within 30 days of your purchase and we'll refund you fully, no questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets through Razorpay. All payments are secure and encrypted with industry-standard SSL.

Technical Questions

Is my data secure? +
Yes! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) to protect your data. All invoices are stored securely in the cloud. We never share your data with third parties. Your WhatsApp messages are processed and immediately deleted from our servers.
What happens to my invoices if I cancel? +
On the Free plan, invoices are stored for 7 days. On paid plans, invoices are stored forever. If you cancel your Pro subscription, you'll move to Free plan, and invoices older than 7 days will be archived (not deleted). If you resubscribe, all your data is restored.
Can I export my data? +
Yes! From your dashboard, you can download all your invoices as PDFs or export your invoice data as CSV for use in Excel or accounting software.
Do you have an API? +
API access is available on the Business plan. This lets you integrate InvoiceEase with your own tools, CRM, or accounting software. Contact us for API documentation.
