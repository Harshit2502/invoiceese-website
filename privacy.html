Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

At InvoiceEase, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

TL;DR: We collect only the data needed to provide the service. We don't sell your data. We use encryption to keep it safe. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you sign up and use InvoiceEase, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, WhatsApp number, password (encrypted)
  • Business Details: Business name, GST number, PAN, address, city, state, pincode
  • Bank Information: Bank name, account number, IFSC code, UPI ID (optional)
  • Invoice Data: Client names, amounts, service descriptions, invoice details you create
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by Razorpay)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • WhatsApp Messages: Messages you send us are processed to generate invoices, then immediately deleted
  • Cookies: We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Purpose Data Used
Provide the service All account and invoice data
Generate invoices Business details, client info, amounts
Process payments Payment method, billing address
Send service updates Email address
Customer support Account info, usage history
Improve the product Usage patterns (anonymized)
Prevent fraud IP address, device info

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Razorpay: Payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
  • WhatsApp/Gupshup: Message delivery (messages are encrypted and deleted immediately)
  • AWS/Cloudflare: Cloud hosting and file storage
  • OpenAI: Invoice parsing (we send only invoice text, not your business details)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Protect user safety

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Database: Hosted on secure servers in India or Singapore
  • Invoice PDFs: Stored on Cloudflare R2 or AWS S3
  • Backups: Encrypted daily backups for disaster recovery

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: 256-bit SSL encryption for data in transit
  • Database Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted at rest
  • Access Control: Strict employee access policies
  • WhatsApp Messages: Processed in memory, never stored on disk
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Available on Pro and Business plans

4.3 Data Retention

  • Free Plan: Invoices stored for 7 days
  • Paid Plans: Invoices stored indefinitely while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted after 30 days
  • WhatsApp Messages: Deleted immediately after processing

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Download

You can access, view, and download all your data from your dashboard at any time.

5.2 Correction

Update your business information, bank details, or account settings anytime from your dashboard.

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by:

  • Going to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or
  • Emailing privacy@invoiceease.in

All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

5.4 Data Portability

Export all your invoices as PDF or CSV from your dashboard.

5.5 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send. You'll still receive important service updates.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Keep you logged in, remember preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how you use the service (anonymized)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We don't track you for ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. Third-Party Services

InvoiceEase integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • WhatsApp: See Meta's Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: See Razorpay's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: We send only invoice text for parsing, see OpenAI's Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

InvoiceEase is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we've collected data from a child, contact us immediately at privacy@invoiceease.in.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in India. If you access InvoiceEase from outside India, your data may be transferred to and stored in India. By using the service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of material changes via:

  • Email to your registered email address
  • Notice on our website
  • In-app notification

Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
